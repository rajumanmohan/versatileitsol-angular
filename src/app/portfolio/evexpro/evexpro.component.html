Evexpro

Evexpro, an App from Emm Dee Inc, is an end-to- end solution for the companies and individuals who want to organize, both corporate and public, Events or Exhibitions.The app has three categories: Company, Agency, Fabricator.

Objective

When a Company has a requirement of verticals in an Exhibition or organize in-house events, they can just post their requirements in this App once the registration process is completed. There are no limitations on number of events to be organized or exhibitions in which they participate. Once the requirement is submitted, it is transferred to a domain of Agency. The registered Agencies will be intimated about the Company’s requirement. The quotation and the designs for this requirement will be submitted by the Agencies to the app. The company can opt from the top 5 quotations from the Agencies. Depending on the Company’s choice the Agency or Agencies will be intimated through mail and pop up notes about their selection. Similarly, the registered Fabricators will also be in the process. There are also opportunities for the companies to exhibit their products through the advertisements.
