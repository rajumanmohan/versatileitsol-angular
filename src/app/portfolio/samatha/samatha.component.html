Samatha

A statewide initiative to empower various stakeholders at all levels to take action in aiding children to lead a healthy and positive future. Samatha App, is a framework that simplifies and assists every team member perform their job of fighting Anaemia.

Objective

Now, an ANM working at the ground level is empowered with a simplified tool that collates each child's information which can be filtered in to multiple aspects of information. Be it getting to know the number of children that are anaemic in a school, village or mandal; it can at the click of a button, it can also give you precise information of a particular child, his/her anaemia level, name, address, village, etc. The Samatha App can also identify patterns as well, thereby helping understand and take the best corrective action. This application, creates and delivers all required workflow reports that shall help all Health and Administrative stakeholders of the state of Telangana combat and win the battle of Anaemia. Positive push notifications guide the ANMs achieve their goals sooner by clearly showing the precise child and his/her location where care has to be administered. Such specific information coupled with mentoring and monitoring of state health administrators will surely improve the current scenario and up the health bar of the state.
