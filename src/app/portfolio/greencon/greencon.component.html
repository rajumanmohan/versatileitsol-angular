Green Connexion

Objective

Main motive of this App is that Village gets a daily supply of water and this is distributed from the supplier to the houses. Supplier supplies water to distributor which delivers to some houses that are assigned to him daily. Assigned delivery boy must register the user with household details and picture of owner & Toilets by integrating with QR code with water meter. Outflow of water would be measured by water meter at each house, where main meter will be assigned to distributor and supplier.

The overall objectives of the project are:

  • Distributor logins into the app and Registers a house and recharges the Smart Card.
  • Distributor will enter the number of litres of water and confirms the delivery.
  • A pin must enter to confirm the delivery. Then they will scan the smart card to deduct payment.
