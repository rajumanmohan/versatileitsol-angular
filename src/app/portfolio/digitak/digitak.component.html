Digitak

Objective

Digitak App give all Swedes the opportunity to get a quote on a roof exchange of a villa, townhouse or tenant-owner association in less than 30 seconds. This not only simplifies the purchase, quotation and decision-making process for buyers, but also for the craftsmen who carry out the work.

How it works

  • Highlight your roof : Enter your address and mark out all the corners of the roof with the help of our roof calculation application.
  • Choose your roof design : Choose the image of the roof design closest to your house.
  • Choose your material : Choose the material you are interested in for your roofing Your price estimate is complete .

Digitak's pricing algorithm takes into account the following parameters

  • Size
  • Design
  • Desired material

The price is available directly on the screen.
