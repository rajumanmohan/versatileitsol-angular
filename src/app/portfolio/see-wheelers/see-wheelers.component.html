SEE Wheelers

SEE Wheelers is a social networking app with sustainability as its central, is user specific about the Genre the user wants to explore.

Objective

SEE stands for ‘Social’, ‘Economical’ and ‘Environmental’ (considered to be the three pillars of sustainability) that together form the basis for this product/service. On the outset, SEE Wheelers is a mobile-based application (with a global outreach) that acts as a social networking platform for all the sustainability-conscious individuals as well as companies and enables them further to be able to create and own an ‘online sustainability footprint’ (i.e., the wheeler). The objective of creating the online sustainability footprint is to produce and deliver ‘impact’.

The online sustainability footprint is created based on the users choice of sustainability dimensions (Ex - Recycling, Plasticless living, Health & wellness etc.,). Using the online sustainability footprint, users will not only be able to create useful content (feeding the wheeler) based on the dimensions selected for their footprints but also be able to build a network of individuals and companies that share a common concern(s)/dimensions of sustainability.

The overall aim of the platform is to bring people, firms, and other types of entities together to create, share, and discuss the ideas/thoughts, actions, and achievements that have everything to do with sustainability. Majority of the dimensions of Social, Economical, and Environmental sustainability are being addressed in the application. These three areas are subdivided into different dimensions wherein users can pick and choose the dimensions of their interest (the dimensions that the users are most connected to). This leads to the creation of ‘online sustainability footprint’.
