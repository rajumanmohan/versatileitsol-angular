India-CME

We at Indiacme is a group of young like minded people working towards the cause of having a single window to access CME program in India. We aim at helping the doctor fraternity in India to earn and manage CME points.

Objective

India-CME platform facilitate a doctor to save his important records such as registration certificates, participation documents etc., IndiaCME aims at covering major events across India by uniting the major stakeholders Doctors, Association, Drug Marketing Companies, Medical Council and MCI. As we look ahead for 2017, it gives a sense of optimism and positivity because healthcare industry and stand against various storm and is self resistance against change.
