Vortex

Vortex will be used by customers of Leila’s Skin Care and foot Clinic, Stockholm, Sweden.

Objective

Vortex will be used by customers of Leila’s Skin Care and foot Clinic, Stockholm, Sweden. With this app, clients can chat with clinic to get to know about different procedures and treatments. Clients can also schedule a time to see the orthopedician. When chatting with the client, the clinic automatically knows who they are speaking with, so that they can get directly to the point.

Main Features

  • Create Profile
  • Chat with clinic
  • Schedule appointment.
