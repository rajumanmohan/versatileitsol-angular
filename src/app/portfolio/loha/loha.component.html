LOHA

Due to globalization and fast changing communication system, there was a need of platform to meet buyers and suppliers of their respective fields. LOHA is started by professionals who have experience in manufacturing and procurement.

Objective

Loha App is used to Trade materials Like, All types of ferrous scrap; TMT; billets & ingots, All types of nonferrous scrap & ingots, Ferro alloys, Coal; all types coke; ore and minerals. Also one can get the prices which are posted daily on the APP for materials such as : All types of nonferrous metals & alloys, HMS (80:20); end cutting & ship breaking, steel & foundry grade pig iron, TMT Bar; ingots; billets .With the same login password, one can use android; iPhone & web browser. Those who have business license / VAT / GST are only allowed to use LOHA. Presently LOHA is free for commercial transactions. One can post Ads at free of cost in web browser.
