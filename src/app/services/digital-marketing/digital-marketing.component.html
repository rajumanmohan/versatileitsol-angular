Pay per click

Pay Per Click is a new-age method of marketing wherein we attract customers through paid and sponsored content on search engines. PPC requires a lot of strategic planning in terms of landing pages, content, A/B testing and finally, targeting. All this requires experts to conceptualize, manage and run the campaign.

PPC advertising is a strategic and yet effective way to earn a spot at the top of Google and Bing search results as well as on websites and digital platforms.

Types of Pay Per Click Advertising

  • Google AdWords : Once we understand your business, we help you gain quick visibility within your target market. As your potential customer types in the keywords, they will be shown landing pages and website pages from your site that will encourage them to click through.
  • Facebook Advertising : With Facebook's advanced marketing and ad tools, it is a great way to drive traffic to your page - here, with targeted ads, messaging and graphics - we reach your customers where they are, not where we are. This entices them to click through.
  • Display Advertising : If you're looking for reach and a large marketing campaign to generate brand awareness- display advertising via Google is for you.
  • Remarketing : This is a way to engage people who've previously spent time on your site. Here you can cross-sell, upsell, remind or incentivise them.
