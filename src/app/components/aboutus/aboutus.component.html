Our History


Starting out is always an adventure and a walk on the wild side! Started in 2012, by 2013, Versatile’s mission was to be the one-stop shop for businesses, startup till large enterprise. We began with the basics, so as to soak our feet in the industry, and month over month, year over year, kept adding to the list of services offered.

Just as our services grew, so did our employees, obviously. What started off as 5 people, by 2014 became 20+ people. We even outgrew our office! This was just phase one in Versatile’s growth story.

Phase two began like a rocket! We were versed with the market and it’s requirements. By 2014 we had 40+ stable clients who trusted us with all their tech and marketing needs. For most, we worked end-to-end, solving challenges and boosting their business.

As of 2017, we are proud to claim we were a team of 50+ people. That’s over an 100% growth! Our team came from all parts of UAE and are the best in field and domain. We hired some amazing people during this time who were multi-faceted and talented- we are proud of our team!

Since the beginning of 2018, we’ve had many exciting developments at Versatile - from 2012 to 2017 till now. The team is now more proficient and efficient than ever! We are looking to continue building relationships and technical strengths with you, our esteemed customers.

From a 5 people team with a vision, we are now a team of 70+ working to change the technology space!
