Srinivas Mogilipala

Managing Director & CEO

Mr.Srinivas Mogilipala is the Managing Director and CEO of Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd located in Hyderabad, India. Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd was started in 2012 with 5 members and a vision to be a comprehensive solution for companies of all sizes. 

With just 5 members, Mr.Srinivas Mogilipala invested time into discovering their strengths, then, trained them,and sharpened their skillset and to ensure everyone was in fact industry ready.

Mr. Srinivas Mogilipala is also the MD of Versatile IT Solutions LLC, Dubai and Director of Value Global Services, Malaysia.

His vision to change the way companies engage service providers has set the precedent for Versatile for today and the years to come. As Versatile’s team grew over the years, we are currently 70+ employees and our turnover has peaked above 10CR.

Before starting Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd, Mr.Srinivas Mogilipala was a skilled UI developer at MayBank in Kuala Lumpur. Before working within design, Mr. Mogilipala was engaged as a Senior Mobility Developer at Cognizant Technology Solutions. His in-depth knowledge within design and development helped him to visualize the steps to fulfilling an even bigger dream.

Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd is creating the future, we are the one-stop solution for companies looking for an integrated all-rounded solution provider.
